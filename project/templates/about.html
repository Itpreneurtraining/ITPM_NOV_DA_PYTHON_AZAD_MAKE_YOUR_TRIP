About MakeUrTrip

MakeUrTrip has designed its logo by taking inspiration from the bird popularly called as ‘Asian Paradise fly catcher’. The bird is aptly described in Indian parlance as ‘heavenly dancer’ (स्वर्गीय नर्तक) because it is known for its unique free-wheeling attributes, soaring and whirling skills and rhythmic prancing. Travonika, alike this exclusive bird, is regarded for passionate service and exciting tour operations across the world. Stimulating adaptation is done to improvise the logo design by aesthetically decorating the elongated tail and colourful plumage.
